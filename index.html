Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore retail data to identify sales trends, profitability drivers, and regional performance gaps. The main objective: transform raw transaction data into business insights that support data-driven decisions.

Key Findings

Category Performance

Technology: Highest sales revenue & profit. Clear winner.

Office Supplies: Strong profitability with healthy margins.

Furniture: High sales volume but relatively low profit. Potential margin issue.

Regional Performance

West Region: Highest sales revenue. Key growth driver.

South Region: Lowest sales revenue. Needs strategic attention.

Business Recommendations

  • 1. Double Down on Technology: Expand product offerings and marketing spend to maximize revenue and profit.
  • 2. Fix Furniture Margins: Review pricing, discount strategies, and operational costs. High sales ≠ high profit.
  • 3. Boost the South Region: Strengthen marketing and sales initiatives to close the revenue gap.
  • 4. Invest in Winners: Continue investing in West and East regions to sustain growth.
  • 5. Track Profitability: Monitor category-level profit margins monthly for sustainable growth.
